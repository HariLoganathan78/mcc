💠 CGPA, GPA & Credit Systems

GPA is the average of grade points for one semester. CGPA is the cumulative GPA of all semesters. Credit indicates the weight of a subject.

📆 Academic Calendar & Attendance

Semester schedules are posted on the college portal. Minimum 75% attendance is required for exam eligibility.

🔗 College Rules, ID & Portal

Students must wear ID cards, follow dress code, and log in to the student portal regularly for updates, marks, and fees.
